About the Project

CADALOC stands for Archaeothanatological Re-evaluation of the Burial in the Roman Flag Sanctuary (aedes) of Augustianis at the Later Town Parish Church's Crypt (Traismauer, Austria). The project investigates a burial found in the crypt of the town parish church of Traismauer (Austria). It applies archaeothanatological, anthropological, archaeogenetic, and radiocarbon dating methods to re-evaluate previous findings and enhance our understanding of the historical context.
