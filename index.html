About the Project

CADALOC stands for archaeothanatologiCAl re-evaluation of the burial in the roman flag sanctuary (aeDes) of Augustianis at the Later tOwn parish church's Crypt (Traismauer/Austria). The project investigates a burial found in the crypt of the town parish church of Traismauer (Austria). It applies archaeothanatological, anthropological, archaeogenetic, and 14C-dating methods to re-evaluate previous findings and enhance our understanding of the historical context.
